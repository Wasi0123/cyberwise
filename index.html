Welcome to CyberWise

Empowering older adults with clear, accessible, and practical cybersecurity education. Learn how to stay safe online, recognise scams, and build digital confidence in a secure environment.

About the Project

CyberWise is a purpose-built cybersecurity education platform designed specifically for older users. This MSc project explores how inclusive design, usable security principles, and accessibility best practices can improve how cybersecurity concepts are communicated and understood. The platform focuses on practical learning, simple explanations, and interactive guidance tailored to the needs of older adults.

Phishing & Scam Awareness

Learn how to identify fraudulent emails, messages, and websites designed to steal personal or financial information.

Password & Account Security

Understand how to create strong passwords, protect accounts, and use authentication methods safely.

Safe Browsing Practices

Discover how to navigate websites securely, recognise warning signs, and avoid common online risks.

Accessible & Inclusive Design

Built with readability, clarity, and simplicity in mind to support users with different abilities and levels of digital experience.
