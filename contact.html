Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will you respond to my message?
We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours during weekdays. Messages sent on weekends will be answered on the following Monday.
Can I get help with a specific module?
Absolutely! Please select "Module Question" from the topic dropdown and include the module name in your message. We're happy to help clarify any concepts.
Is phone support available for technical issues?
Yes, phone support is available during office hours for urgent technical issues. For non-urgent matters, email is usually more efficient.
What happens to my personal information?
Your data is stored securely and only used to respond to your enquiry. We never share your information with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.
